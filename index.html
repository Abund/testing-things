HealthCare

Goal Of the Final app

this application is one that helps users keep track of there health conditions by enabling them monitor certain health conditions such as blood preessure,blood sugar, and calorie intake. the application will also provide the users with helpful resoursces such as sections in medical jornals and various online contents that will help the give the user advice on handling blood sugar,blood pressur and calories should any of this conditions be on the high. A primary persona of this application is peter okoye a 65 year old retiree in Nigeria currently diagnosed off diabeties who plans to monitor his sugar and calorie intake. peter will be able to view and monitor the results on a daily,weekly and monthly basis. this will enable peter set goals and monitor it.


Summary of goals:
  • help users keep track of their health conditions such as blood sugar,pressure and calories over time
  • provide useful tips to users to enable them stay healthy
  • remind them on taking there medication
  • Provide a minimalistic interface for fast direct usage.
